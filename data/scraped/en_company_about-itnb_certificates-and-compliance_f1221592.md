## Certifications and Compliance
The provision of software, hardware, cybersecurity applicartion - and maintenance from a single source guarantees unparalleled reliability, security and efficiency. We partnered with Phoenix Technologies, which infrastructure complies with ISO 27001 and is audited according to ISAE 3402.

### ISO 27001
We have implemented quality management processes for information security based on the international standard ISO 27001. This ensures the highest level of security for all assets we own.

### ISAE 3402 Type II
ISAE 3402 type 2 compliance means that we consistently deliver AI-cloud and confidential cloud solutions that meet the highest standards of security, data privacy, and compliance in Switzerland.
